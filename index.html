Introduction

Karise Arts Studio is located 25 kilometers east of Harare opposite the National Rehabilitation Center in Ruwa, Zimbabwe. Karise arts has been in existence for over 2 decades and prides itself by being one of the best when it comes to stone sculpture. The studio was established in 2000 by Luxon Karise and has also been pivotal for emerging stone sculpturers. The gallery hosts a large variety of quality stone pieces that reflect the history, craftsmanship and lifestyles of the Zimbabwean people all crafted from locally available natural stones.

At Karise Arts Studio, we curate the finest natural stones pieces from Zimbabwe.
